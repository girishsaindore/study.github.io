Text Formating Tag

  1. HEADING

There Are 6 Tags Of Heading.

  • < h1 > < /h1 >
  • < h2 > < /h2 >
  • < h3 > < /h3 >
  • < h4 > < /h4 >
  • < h5 > < /h5 >
  • < h6 > < /h6 >
  1. PARAGRAPH

Example:-   < p > my name is Girish Saindore < /p >

  1. BOLD

Example:- < b > My Name Is Girish Saindore < /b >

  1. ITALIC

Example:- < i > My Name Is Girish Saindore < /i >

  1. UNDERLINE

Example:- < u > My Name Is Girish Saindore < /u >

  1. STRICKTHROUGHT

Example:- < s > My Name Is Girish Saindore < /s >

  1. SUB-SCRIPT

Example:- H < sub > 2 < /sub > o

  1. SUPER-SCRIPT

Example:- < sup > < /sub >

  1. BREAK

Example:- My Name Is Girish < br > From:-Manjari.

  1. NON BREAKING SPACE

< Tag:- & nbsp ; >

This Tag Using To Some Space Into Two Words.

Example:- Girish   Saindore
